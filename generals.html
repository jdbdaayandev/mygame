How to Play

Setup Phase: Before the battle begins, your pieces are placed in the bottom 3 rows. Click any two pieces (or an empty square) in your zone to swap their positions. Click "Ready" when done.

Objective: Capture the opponent's Flag, or safely maneuver your Flag to the opposite end of the board.

Movement

  • You can move one piece per turn.
  • Pieces can move one square in any direction: forward, backward, left, right, or diagonally.

Combat & The Arbiter

  • When your piece attacks an enemy piece, the Arbiter resolves the battle automatically.
  • The higher-ranked piece wins and eliminates the lower-ranked piece.
  • If both pieces have the same rank, both are eliminated.
  • The opponent's rank remains hidden unless you tie and both pieces die!
